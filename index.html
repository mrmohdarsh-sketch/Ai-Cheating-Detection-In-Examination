Problem Statement & Objective

Online exams face massive challenges like Impersonation, Secondary Device usage, and Peer Collaboration. Traditional proctoring is expensive and prone to human error.

Our Solution:

  • Automated Monitoring: 24/7 surveillance without human fatigue.
  • Identity Integrity: Constant face-matching throughout the session.
  • Scalability: One system can monitor thousands of students simultaneously.

System ka main goal hai ek Fair Testing Environment create karna jaha AI har ek frame ko analyze karke suspicious activity ko flag kare.

Module 1: Visual Intelligence (CV)

Hum YOLOv8 (You Only Look Once) model ka use kar rahe hain. Ye model image ko grid mein divide karta hai aur ek hi pass mein bounding boxes aur class probabilities calculate karta hai.

DETECTED: MOBILE PHONE (99.2%)

Technical Specs:

  • Gaze Tracking: Eye-ball movement analysis using 68-point facial landmarks.
  • Multi-Face Detection: Detecting if more than one person is visible in the frame.
  • Head Pose: Pitch, Yaw, and Roll calculation to detect side-glances.

Module 2: Audio & Behavioral Analytics

Sirf video kafi nahi hai, isliye hum audio frequencies ko bhi scan karte hain. Voice Activity Detection (VAD) ka use karke background noise aur human whisper ke beech ka farq samjha jata hai.

Behavioral Biometrics:

  • Keystroke Dynamics: Analyzing the rhythm of typing to detect external help.
  • Tab Switching: Detecting if the browser focus is shifted to other applications.
  • NLP Analysis: Converting audio to text to find "Keywords" like "Answer", "Help", "Option".

The Architecture & Future Scope

Ye system FastAPI backend aur TensorFlow/PyTorch models par chalta hai. Har violation ka ek score generate hota hai, agar score 80% se upar jaye toh student ko warning di jati hai.

Future Enhancements:

  • Edge AI: Running models locally on the user's browser via WebAssembly to save bandwidth.
  • Anti-Deepfake: Detecting AI-generated video loops used for cheating.
  • Emotion Analysis: Detecting stress or unusual anxiety levels.
